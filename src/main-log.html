Чем “Легенды Древних” отличается от других игр?

10.10.201518:50

The very best way to spend your New Year's Eve in Budapest is definitely on the magnificent river, with the wonderous sight of the celebrating capital.Join the grand event on the river on the 31st of December and celebrate your new year in and unique way! The 'New Year's Eve on the Danube' program is a full night celebration, including a circle on the river, open-buffet dinner plus late night dinner in New Year's Eve style - accompanied by all-night -long open bar, folklore showpiece on board, raffle much more!
